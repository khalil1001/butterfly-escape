Amber Season in Paris

October 11, 2022

← Back to all articles

How is Paris during autumn? Some people would say it’s too gloomy, others would regret summertime… Well, for my part, I love sunny weather and at the same time, I love being here during fall season for many many reasons…

From end of September to mid-November, I mostly enjoy:

  • nature colorful palettes 🍃🍂
  • soft October sun and sunsets ☀️
  • gentle mornings in the park 🐿
  • crunchy leaves all over the neighborhood 🍁
  • ´latte’ dates behind a coffeeshop window on a rainy day🥤
  • cozy sweater evenings at home around a nice book 📖☕️

Isn’t it the most wonderful time of the year ?
